### One template, everyone.

Marketing tools today blast the same email to every customer, built on broad cohorts and segments, not on what any one shopper actually wants.

×

### Generic subject lines.

"We miss you", "Don't miss out", "Last chance". They blend into the inbox and never get opened.

×

### Six or more random products.

A grid of unrelated items in one email creates decision fatigue. Shoppers scroll, shrug, and close the tab.

×

### Discount codes that leak.

Static, easily-shared codes flatten margin and create no real urgency. Bargain hunters apply them; loyal customers never see them.

×

### One-size timing.

Your VIP gets the same Tuesday-10am send as a first-time browser. Sending windows are picked by the marketer, not the buyer.

×

### Channel guesswork.

Email-first, when many of your customers actually respond on WhatsApp or SMS. The channel they prefer is invisible to your stack.

03 · How we do it

## Smart, AI-based outreach personalisation.

Every outreach is composed in real time. The greeting, the products, the context, and even the discount code are generated per customer, not pulled from a template library.

### Deep Personalisation

A personalised entry point. We know exactly who we are talking to and what they have purchased in the past and use that to drive highly relevant product recommendations.

Hi Darryl,

Given your past purchases of Prestige Teal Linen Pants and Inserch SS Abstract Wave Shirt, we think you've got excellent taste.

### Real-time relevance

We weave in real-time location, weather, and seasonality. Every recommendation references the customer's current climate and moment, so it lands the day they actually need it.
