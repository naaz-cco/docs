02 · How llea matches buyers to stuck stock

## Seven matching signals, one matched audience.

Mass discounts assume everyone is the same buyer. The reality is that for any specific SKU, a few hundred shoppers in your existing customer base are the right audience. llea finds them across seven signals, grouped into three streams, and combines the results.

Geographic Match

Brand & Category
