Llea is a customer intelligence platform built exclusively for Shopify brands. Every time someone visits your store, Llea reads over 100 behavioural, transactional, contextual, and demographic signals and turns them into a single purchase-intent score between 0 and 100 — refreshed in real time, invisible to the shopper, and specific to your store. Instead of blasting promotions to everyone and hoping something sticks, you use that score to focus your email, SMS, WhatsApp, and ad spend on the visitors who are actually ready to buy.

## Where Llea Fits in Your Stack

Llea sits between Shopify and your marketing channels. It reads from Shopify, enriches every visitor profile with intent data, and pushes that intelligence downstream to wherever you engage customers.

| Layer | Tool | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Store platform** | Shopify | Orders, products, and customer records live here |
| **Intelligence layer** | Llea | Scores every visitor 0–100 in real time |
| **Engagement channels** | Email · SMS · WhatsApp · Ads | Triggers fire only when intent crosses your threshold |

You do not replace anything in your current stack. Llea adds a scoring and signal layer that makes your existing tools smarter.

## What Llea Is Not

It helps to be clear about what Llea does not do, so you can set the right expectations before you install.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Not a heatmap tool" icon="map">
    Llea does not record screen sessions or generate click heatmaps. It scores purchase intent — not page design quality.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Not a replacement for your CEP" icon="envelope">
    Llea does not send emails or SMS messages itself. It feeds intent scores and signals to Klaviyo, Postscript, Attentive, or whichever platform you already use.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Not a web analytics tool" icon="chart-bar">
    Llea is not a replacement for Google Analytics 4 or similar. You will not find pageview dashboards or traffic acquisition reports here.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Not a black box" icon="eye">
    Every score links back to the individual signals that produced it. You can click any customer profile and see exactly why their score is what it is.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who Llea Is For

Llea is designed for a specific kind of Shopify operator — one who has moved past basic segmentation and wants to act on what each individual shopper is doing right now.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="D2C Brands">
    If your store does between **$50 000 and $5 000 000 per month**, you have enough purchase history for Llea's per-store model to train on, and enough traffic for the intent scores to be statistically meaningful. You will see the clearest lift from suppressing low-intent shoppers and concentrating spend on the top decile.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Agencies">
    If you manage multiple Shopify brands, Llea's per-store architecture means each client gets a model trained exclusively on their own data — never pooled with other merchants. You can manage stores independently from a single account and build intent-triggered flows as a repeatable service offering.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Multi-Category Stores">
    If your catalogue spans multiple product categories, Llea tracks intent at the product and category level independently. A shopper browsing your footwear range and another browsing your outerwear range receive scores that reflect what each of them is actually considering — not a blended signal that obscures both.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What to Expect After You Install

Llea is a maturing intelligence system, not a light switch. Here is what the timeline typically looks like from the moment you complete installation.

<Accordion title="Week 1: Pixel live, backfill complete, first scores">
  Within the first 24 hours, Llea's web pixel is active on your storefront, the last 90 days of order history have been backfilled, and your dashboard shows live intent scores on current visitors. The model is still early — it has limited purchase events to learn from — but scores are already usable for basic intent filtering.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Weeks 4–8: Model maturation">
  As Llea accumulates more purchase events from live traffic, the per-store model matures. Predictions sharpen, the gap between high-intent and low-intent shoppers widens, and you start to see the top decile converting at 4–7× the store average. This is when intent-triggered automations deliver the clearest return.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Ongoing: Continuous learning">
  The model never stops updating. Every purchase, every new browse session, and every channel interaction feeds back into the scoring engine. Seasonal patterns, new product launches, and shifts in your customer mix are all incorporated automatically — no retraining or configuration required on your part.
</Accordion>

<Note>
  Llea requires no theme edits, no code snippets, and no developer involvement. The web pixel is registered automatically during installation and adds less than 50 ms to your Largest Contentful Paint.
</Note>
