Llea slots into your existing marketing stack as an intent-routing brain, not a replacement for the tools you already depend on. Every integration follows the same pattern: your Shopify store feeds behavioural events and order history to Llea, Llea computes a real-time intent score for each shopper, and when that score crosses your configured threshold, it fires a Shopify Flow event that triggers your existing campaigns in whichever platform you use — Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Wati, Postscript, or anything else connected to Flow.

## How the architecture works

Data and decisions move through four stages in sequence:

1. **Shopify Store** — Your storefront emits pixel events (page views, add-to-cart, checkout steps) alongside your historic order data.
2. **Llea (intent scoring)** — Llea ingests those signals and runs a per-store model, producing a live intent score for every active shopper.
3. **Shopify Flow (routing)** — When a shopper's score crosses your threshold, Llea fires a `High Intent Customer` event into Shopify Flow. Flow inspects the payload and routes the trigger to the right channel using its built-in conditions and connectors.
4. **Delivery channels** — Your CEP (Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Wati, Postscript, and so on) receives the trigger and fires your existing campaign template. Nothing in your campaign setup changes — only the audience does.

<Note>
  All integrations require Shopify Flow to be active on your store. Flow is free and available on all Shopify plans — no paid upgrade required.
</Note>

## Multi-store merchants

Install Llea once per store — each store runs its own intent model trained on that store's shoppers and catalog, and gets its own dedicated dashboard. If you manage three or more stores, a roll-up view becomes available in your Llea account so you can compare intent trends and performance across your entire portfolio without switching between dashboards.

<Info>
  Agency and brand-group accounts can request early access to the roll-up view by emailing [support@llea.ai](mailto:support@llea.ai). The roll-up is read-only; each store's settings and thresholds remain independent.
</Info>
