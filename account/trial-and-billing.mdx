Llea's trial and billing are designed to stay entirely within Shopify, so there's no separate account to manage and no payment details to enter with Llea directly. You approve the trial in your Shopify admin, explore the platform at no cost for 14 days, and Shopify handles any charges that follow — exactly like every other app in your stack.

## After the Trial

Once your trial ends, billing transitions automatically through Shopify — there's nothing you need to do to keep Llea running.

- **Base fee:** $0.006 per store visitor, billed monthly.
- **Win fees:** 3% of incremental engagement revenue and 3% of clearance margin saved, billed monthly in arrears.
- **Events:** unlimited after the trial period ends.
- **Invoice location:** Shopify Admin → Settings → Billing & Usage → Apps.

<Info>
Win fees are incremental-only. If Llea doesn't generate a lift in a given month, the win fee for that module is zero. You pay only the base analytics fee.
</Info>

## Cancelling

You can cancel at any time from your Shopify admin — no notice period, no cancellation fee.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Shopify Admin → Settings → Billing & Usage → Apps">
    This is the same place you manage all your Shopify app subscriptions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Llea and click Remove app">
    Shopify will confirm the removal. Billing stops immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data purge begins">
    Llea receives Shopify's `app/uninstalled` webhook and begins deleting your store's data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deletion completes within 48 hours">
    The `shop/redact` webhook from Shopify confirms the final purge of all store-level data from Llea's live database.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Encrypted backups rotate within 30 days">
    Any trace of your data in encrypted backup files is automatically rotated out within 30 days of uninstall.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Cancelling fully deletes your store's data and model. If you reinstall Llea later, the model starts fresh and will need 4–8 weeks to reach steady state again. Consider **pausing** instead if you plan to return.
</Warning>

## Pausing vs Cancelling

If you're heading into a quiet season, pausing is almost always the better choice.

| | **Pause** | **Cancel** |
|---|---|---|
| **Billing** | Stops immediately | Stops immediately |
| **Model state** | Preserved for 90 days | Deleted within 48 hours |
| **Shopper data** | Retained | Purged; backups rotate within 30 days |
| **On return** | Resume instantly, no retraining needed | Fresh install; model retrains from scratch (4–8 weeks to steady state) |
| **How to do it** | Shopify Admin → Billing & Usage → Apps → Pause | Shopify Admin → Billing & Usage → Apps → Remove app |

<Tip>
Pause rather than cancel during low seasons. Your trained model persists for 90 days and you won't lose the 4–8 weeks of learning when you reactivate.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Where do I find my Llea invoices?">
  All Llea charges appear in **Shopify Admin → Settings → Billing & Usage → Apps** alongside your other Shopify app charges. Llea never sends separate invoices or stores your payment details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I switch from Pay As You Go to Enterprise?">
  Yes. Contact [admin@llea.ai](mailto:admin@llea.ai) to discuss an Enterprise plan. We'll coordinate the transition so there's no gap in service or data continuity.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if I reinstall within 48 hours of cancelling?">
  If you reinstall within the 48-hour deletion window, Llea can restore your store's data. After 48 hours, deletion is complete and a reinstall starts fresh.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a setup fee?">
  No. There is no setup fee on any plan. The 14-day trial has no cost at all, and the Pay As You Go plan begins billing only after the trial ends.
</Accordion>

<Note>
Llea never stores your payment details. All billing is handled entirely by Shopify.
</Note>
