Llea's pricing has two layers: a base fee of **$0.006 per store visitor** that covers continuous intent scoring and analytics, plus a **3% win fee** charged only on the incremental revenue and margin that Llea's outreach actually generates. Because the base fee tracks visitor volume — not your store's revenue — your bill scales with platform usage, not your topline. And because win fees are incremental-only, Llea is only rewarded when it moves the needle.

## What Counts as a Win Fee

A win fee applies only to the **incremental** uplift that Llea's outreach generates — the revenue above your pre-Llea baseline.

- **1:1 Personalised Outreach win fee:** 3% of the extra revenue driven by Llea's intent-targeted sends, compared to your unassisted baseline. If Llea didn't send it, the revenue doesn't count.
- **Smart Stock Clearance win fee:** 3% of the markdown margin Llea saves by routing slow-moving stock to high-intent buyers rather than applying a blanket discount.
- **No win = no win fee.** If Llea's outreach generates zero lift in a billing period, the win fee for that module is zero. You pay only the base analytics fee.

<Note>
Pricing is based on visitor volume, not store revenue. If your average order value doubles, your Llea bill doesn't.
</Note>

## Is Llea Right for Your Store?

Answer these three questions honestly before starting your trial. If all three are "yes", run your numbers through the [ROI calculator](https://www.llea.ai/pricing). If two are "yes", a trial is probably worth it. If only one is "yes", wait.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Do you have more than 10,000 monthly visitors?">
    Llea builds a per-store intent model. Below 10,000 monthly visitors, there aren't enough purchase events for the model to score reliably, and the base fee isn't recouped by the win.

    - **Under 10k:** focus on product, photography, and paid acquisition basics first. Come back when traffic grows.
    - **10k–50k:** break-even zone. Worth trialling if your AOV is healthy and you're actively running outreach campaigns.
    - **50k–2M:** the sweet spot. Most stores in this range see 8–15× ROI within the first 90 days.
    - **2M+:** contact sales for an Enterprise flat-rate plan — it's typically a fraction of pay-per-visitor at this scale.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Is your average order value above roughly $50?">
    Win fees are a percentage of incremental revenue. At a very low AOV, even a meaningful conversion lift generates small absolute dollar amounts, making the combined base fee and win fee harder to justify.

    - **AOV ≥ $50:** fee structure works well.
    - **AOV below $50:** consider whether the projected lift covers the base fee before committing past the trial.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Are you actively running email, SMS, or WhatsApp campaigns?">
    Llea's biggest upside comes from sharpening existing outreach — better targeting on campaigns you're already sending. If your marketing budget is entirely in paid ads with no owned-channel outreach, the personalisation lift is smaller.

    - **Running email / SMS / WhatsApp:** full upside available.
    - **Paid ads only:** Llea still delivers analytics and clearance value, but the outreach win fee opportunity is limited.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pausing Your Subscription

You can pause Llea during low seasons without losing your model.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="How to Pause">
    1. Go to **Shopify Admin → Settings → Billing & Usage → Apps**.
    2. Find Llea and select **Pause subscription**.
    3. Billing stops immediately. Your model state is preserved.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="What Happens When Paused">
    - **Billing:** stops the moment you pause.
    - **Model state:** preserved for up to **90 days**.
    - **On reactivation:** no retraining needed. Your intent model picks up where it left off.
    - **After 90 days of pause:** model state expires. A fresh install will retrain from scratch.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Pause vs Cancel">
    | | Pause | Cancel |
    |---|---|---|
    | Billing | Stops | Stops |
    | Model state | Preserved 90 days | Deleted within 48 hours |
    | Data | Retained | Purged (backups rotate within 30 days) |
    | On return | Resume instantly, no retraining | Fresh install, model retrains from scratch |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
Pause rather than cancel during quiet seasons. Your trained model persists and you won't lose the 4–8 weeks of learning when you reactivate.
</Tip>
