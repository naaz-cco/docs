## How Llea fits your stack

Llea does not replace any tool you already use. It adds an intelligence layer that tells your existing tools *who* to act on and *when*.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install from Shopify App Store">
    Approve OAuth scopes (read customers, orders, products + web pixel). No theme edits. No code changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Llea backfills 90 days of data">
    Your per-store intent model trains on your own purchase history overnight. First scores appear within 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your channels">
    Link Klaviyo, WhatsApp, SMS, or any tool via Shopify Flow. Llea fires intent-threshold events; your existing flows and templates do the rest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let personalised outreach run automatically">
    High-intent shoppers receive the right message on their preferred channel at their best-open time — without manual effort.
  </Step>
</Steps>
